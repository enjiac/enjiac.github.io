Stay Authentic

A lot of the time we are unsure of what we want. So we go with the flow. We are told to become a certain way to fit a certain mold. We surrender to a socialized and superficial self. We lose our authenticity in the process. 

A poem by Stephen Crane clicked with me. 

In the desert
I saw a creature, naked, bestial,
Who, squatting upon the ground,
Held his heart in his hands,
And ate of it.
I said, “Is it good, friend?”
“It is bitter—bitter,” he answered;

“But I like it
“Because it is bitter,
“And because it is my heart.”

A road of treachery may lay await for he who follows his heart. But that's okay because it is the path he chose, the path that is his, the path he loves.
